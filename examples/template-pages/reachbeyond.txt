<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong class="myprefix-text-bold"><strong class="myprefix-text-bold">CHURCH PLANTING</strong></strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:yellowtree/row {"template":"1-1","desktopBreakpoint":"md"} -->
<!-- wp:yellowtree/column {"md":6} -->
<!-- wp:image {"id":7104,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://cdn.icf.church/wp-content/live/uploads/sites/1/sites/1/sites/1/2026/02/09010131/kirchenpflanzung-1-1024x918.png" alt="" class="wp-image-7104"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>There are so many places and people in this world who have never heard of Jesus – and many who know Him but cannot find a church that supports and inspires them.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Through your support, new churches are established where people discover faith, flourish, and positively impact their surroundings. Your donation helps the church grow where it is missing – vibrant, accessible, and relevant. </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Today, over <strong>65 ICF churches</strong> have already been established. With your generosity, more will be added – places full of hope, love, and truth. </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Further information: <a href="https://www.icf.church/en/movement/">icf.church/movement </a> </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->

<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong class="myprefix-text-bold"><strong class="myprefix-text-bold"><strong class="myprefix-text-bold">TRAINING PASTORS &amp; KEY LEADERS</strong></strong></strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:yellowtree/column {"md":6} -->
<!-- wp:image {"id":7147,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://cdn.icf.church/wp-content/live/uploads/sites/1/sites/1/sites/1/2026/02/09092410/pklt-2-1024x919.png" alt="" class="wp-image-7147"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong>Everything rises and falls with healthy leadership.</strong> Without well-trained pastors and leaders, strong, healthy churches cannot exist – and without strong local churches, there is no hope for this world.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>That is why we invest specifically in leaders who combine calling, character, and competence. Through the <strong>Pastors &amp; Key Leaders Training</strong>, they are empowered to lead holistically, develop people, and build God's kingdom. </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Last year, we were able to equip over <strong>100 pastors and key leaders</strong> from more than <strong>20 ICF churches</strong>. Over <strong>200 more aspiring leaders</strong> attended the <strong>ICF Experience</strong>, our bootcamp for key people in our ICFs. </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>With your generosity, we are further developing these trainings, translating them into new languages, and making them accessible to even more people – so that leaders grow up everywhere, awakening hope, taking responsibility, and changing lives.</p>
